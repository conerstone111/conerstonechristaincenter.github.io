Founding Pastors

Pastor Samuel Dove is the visionary and the founder of Cornerstone Christian Centre which started on the 12th of June 2016. He was called by God to win souls for Jesus Christ and raise them as true disciples. God has given him an apostolic calling. He is known to move in the prophetic teachings and preaching the word with authority and power. Pastor Samuel is also known for strongly believing in a practical gospel, that the word must be demonstrated practically. Pastor Samuel was brought up in Meadowlands Soweto; he is married to Pastor Maleepile Dove and God has blessed them with a son Bohlale Dove.

Pastor Samuel Dove believes that Jesus is the answer to all problems that people are facing and thus his passion in preaching and teaching the word of God, planting churches and equipping the Saints for the work of the ministry. Connect with Pastor Samuel Dove on Facebook.
