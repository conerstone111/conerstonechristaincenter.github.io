Meet Our Pastors

Pastor Samuel Dove is founder and Lead Pastor of Cornerstone Christian Centre, which was established in June of 2016. His calling and passion is to win people to Christ, bring believers into full maturity of their walk with God, train, raise and mentor leaders in the church by preaching a practical gospel.

Pastor Samuel Dove was brought up in Soweto and is married to Pastor Maleepile Dove...
