Meet Our Pastors

Pastor Samuel & Pastor Maleepile Dove

Pastor Samuel Dove is the visionary and the founder of Cornerstone Christian Centre which started on the 12th of June 2016. He was called by God to win souls for Jesus Christ and raise them as true disciples. God has given him an apostolic calling. He is known to move in the prophetic teachings and preaching the word with authority and power. Pastor Samuel is also known for strongly believing in a practical gospel, that the word must be demonstrated practically.

Pastor Samuel was brought up in Meadowlands Soweto; he is married to Pastor Maleepile Dove...
